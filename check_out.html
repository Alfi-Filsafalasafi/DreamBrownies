Steps for Payment

  1. Fill out the order form, including your personal information and preferences.
  2. Select your desired shipping method from the available options.
  3. Choose your payment method (Credit Card, Bank Transfer, or Gopay).
  4. Input the voucher code to get a discount
  5. Review your order to ensure all details are correct.
  6. Click on the "Send Order" button to submit your order.
  7. Follow the instructions for completing your payment based on the selected method.
  8. Wait for the confirmation by Whatsapp that includes your receipt and shipping details.
